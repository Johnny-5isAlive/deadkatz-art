The Process

How chaos becomes permanent

From Destruction to Resin

Each Dead Katz piece begins with broken things—shattered glass, burnt wood, crushed metal, dried flowers. Materials that have reached their end.

Layer by layer, epoxy resin suspends these fragments in eternal amber. What dies is reborn solid, glossy, immortal. No two pieces share the same DNA.

This is art for the afterlife. Nine lives in every pour.
