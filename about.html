The Process

How chaos becomes permanent

From Destruction to Resin

Each DeadKatz piece begins with a mold and mission.Resin is the material. Built in layers that range from-transparent, tinted, metallic, glow in the dark

to translucent or multi-colored. No two pieces share the same DNA.Some pours take 3 days, some take 7. The wait is part of the process.Never mass produced

Every piece is hand poured, polished, and signed. The flaws are intentional and add unique individual characteristics that ensure each piece is one of a kind.
